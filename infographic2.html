Master Your Action Points (AP)

Your staff's daily output is measured in AP. Every order costs AP to fulfill. No AP, no sales!

Specialists (Cooks, Cashiers) provide focused AP, while Managers provide flexible **General AP** that can be used for any task.

Know Your Menu

Balance high-profit items with popular crowd-pleasers to maximize your daily revenue.

The bubble's size shows popularity, while its position shows price. Find the sweet spot!
