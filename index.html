Your Most Valuable Asset

You can't do it alone! Each employee generates Action Points (AP), the resource needed to get things done. Choosing the right team is key to success.

This chart shows the starting AP for different roles. Managers are flexible, while others are specialists.

The Action Point Economy

Every order costs AP to complete. You need Cooking AP to make the food, and either Service AP (for dine-in) or Delivery AP to get it to the customer. Run out of AP, and you'll miss sales!

Cooks, Cashiers, Drivers→Generate Specialist AP
Managers→Generate General AP
Specialist + General AP→Fulfill Customer Orders

Managers are your wildcards! Their General AP can be used to cover any task, making them essential for a smooth operation.

What's On The Menu?

Not all dishes are created equal. Some are cheap and popular, while others are expensive but ordered less frequently. Balancing your menu and inventory based on price vs. popularity is crucial for maximizing profit.

The size of the bubble indicates its popularity. Pizza is a crowd-pleaser, but the Gourmet Pizza is a rare, high-profit sale.
