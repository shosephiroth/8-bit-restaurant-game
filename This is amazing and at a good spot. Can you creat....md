# **8-Bit Restaurant Manager: Instruction Manual**

Welcome to 8-Bit Restaurant Manager\! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to turn your humble eatery into a culinary empire.

## **🚀 Getting Started**

Your goal is to run a successful restaurant by managing finances, staff, and inventory. You start with **$5000** and a single **Manager**. Your objective is to grow your cash reserves and build a profitable business. If your cash drops below zero, it's game over\!

## **🕹️ Core Gameplay Loop**

The game progresses day by day. Here’s what a typical day looks like:

1. **Prepare for the Day:** Before starting, you can hire/fire staff, purchase ingredients, and adjust your restaurant's settings for the upcoming day.  
2. **Start the Day:** Click the **"Start Next Day"** button to begin the simulation. Your daily expenses (like salaries) will be deducted immediately.  
3. **Serve Customers:** Throughout the day, customers will arrive and place orders. Your staff will automatically try to fulfill these orders based on your available ingredients and **Action Points (AP)**.  
4. **Review Performance:** At the end of the day, a summary will show your total revenue, expenses, and other key metrics. Use this information to plan for the next day.

## **📊 Understanding the UI**

### **Main Stats Bar (Top)**

This sticky bar keeps your most vital stats in view at all times:

* **Day:** The current day of operation.  
* **Cash:** Your current money on hand.  
* **Revenue:** Total income for the current day.  
* **Expenses:** Total costs for the current day, including salaries and purchases.

### **Previous Day Summary**

This panel shows a quick recap of your last day's performance, including customers served, deliveries made, and Action Points used.

### **Daily Capacity (Action Points)**

This is the strategic heart of your restaurant. Your employees generate **Action Points (AP)** that are spent to perform tasks.

* **Cooking AP:** Required to prepare any dish.  
* **Service AP:** Required for in-house customer orders.  
* **Delivery AP:** Required for delivery orders.  
* **General AP:** A flexible pool generated by managers that can be used for any task.  
* **Total AP:** The sum of all available points for the day.

### **Menu**

Displays all the food items you have unlocked. Each item shows its selling price and popularity score, which affects how often it's ordered.

### **Manage Employees**

Here you can hire and fire your staff. Each employee type has a different salary and contributes different types of Action Points. Employees also gain experience and level up over time, becoming more effective.

### **Ingredient Stock & Daily Ledger**

* **Stock:** Shows your current inventory levels. You can buy ingredients in increments of 10, 100, or a custom amount.  
* **Ledger:** Acts as a daily receipt, tracking all your spending on ingredients, upgrades, and marketing, as well as your labor costs.

### **Restaurant Settings & Upgrades**

* **Settings:** Use the slider to set the maximum number of customers you want to attract on the next day.  
* **Upgrades:** Purchase permanent upgrades like a **Better Oven** (which reduces ingredient costs for baked goods) or temporary boosts like a **Marketing Campaign** (which increases customer traffic for 3 days).

### **Actions**

This panel contains key game functions:

* **Start Next Day:** Begins the daily simulation.  
* **View JSON Log:** Opens a detailed, comprehensive log of every action in the game.  
* **Export/Import Save:** Allows you to save your game to a file or load a previous save.  
* **Reset Game:** Starts the game over from the beginning.

## **🏆 Winning the Game**

To win, you need to grow your restaurant's cash reserves to **$25,000**. To succeed, you'll need to strategically balance your staffing, inventory, and daily customer flow to maximize your profits. Good luck\!
